# Village & Pangarh Excursion

### Duration
**Full Day**
- Safari by walk and track: Approximately 4 to 5 hours

### Minimum Group Size
**6 Guests**

---

## Itinerary

### Departure
The tour begins with a **bus / own vehicle departure from Rawat Ka Talab**.

### Village Visit
Upon arrival, guests will:
- Visit a traditional village.
- Explore the surrounding agricultural fields and farms.

This region is well known for **opium cultivation**.

During the **opium harvesting season (December to the end of January)**, guests may have the opportunity to observe opium farming activities, **subject to seasonal availability**.

### Village School Visit *(Subject to Availability)*
If the visit coincides with **school hours** and permission is available, the group will also visit a **local village school** *(subject to school timings).*

### Walking Safari
The safari experience will be conducted **on foot and by track**, covering the natural surroundings and village landscapes.

**Duration:** Approximately **4 to 5 hours**

### Pangarh Visit
The journey then continues through the **scenic Vindhya Valley forest area** towards **Pangarh**.

#### If Lunch is Arranged
- The group will enjoy **lunch at Pangarh**.

#### If Lunch is Not Included
- The group will visit **Pangarh Lake**.
- Followed by **high tea**.

### Return Journey
After the visit, guests will drive to **Bijaipur** by **bus or cab**.

During the journey:
- Hotel jeep
- Hotel staff
- Driver

will join and assist the group.
