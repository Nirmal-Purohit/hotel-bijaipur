Welcome to Castle Bijaipur

Nestled in the scenic Vindhya Valley of Rajasthan, Castle Bijaipur is a heritage property that offers an authentic royal experience. Set amidst rolling hills, lush farmlands, and rich cultural landscapes, our palace invites you to step back in time while enjoying modern comforts.

From sunrise yoga sessions to thrilling jeep safaris, from hands-on cooking classes to serene cycling trails — every moment at Bijaipur is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime.

Bijaipur Horse Safari

Established in the late eighties by the owners of Castle Bijaipur, the Bijaipur Horse Safari offers exclusive horseback holidays through the countryside of South Rajasthan (Mewar). From humble safari tents under the starlit sky to the sophisticated comforts of the Castle — an unforgettable ride through India's heartland.

Sample Itinerary — Beginner Style (9 Days)

Day 01: Arrive Bijaipur before lunch. Traditional welcome and check-in. Evening: first ride (2-hour local ride for experienced riders, or lesson in castle grounds for beginners). Dinner & overnight at Castle Bijaipur.
Day 02–04: Ride mornings & evenings with free afternoons. Expert guides give lessons and take beginners into the countryside along old farm tracks. Experienced riders go further afield. Afternoons: cycling, cooking lessons, massage, pool. Evenings: dine under the stars. Village tours and picnic outings in vintage jeeps.
Day 05: Ride Bijaipur to Motipura (20 km, 5–6 hrs). Through Eastern Highlands with lush green fields amidst hills. Lunch at Damru. Ride through Guner flatlands. Overnight in Mughal-style safari tents at Motipura Lake.
Day 06: Ride Motipura to Pangarh (21 km, 7 hrs). South through the least inhabited area. Lunch at old lake of Rawat. Forests, deserted village of Bhagwanpura. Arrive at Pangarh Lake — a 1000-year-old manmade lake with fort ruins and temples. Birdwatcher's paradise. Overnight at Pangarh Lake Retreat.
Day 07: Ride Pangarh to Bijaipur (23 km, 6 hrs). Through valleys moving north, amazing riding grounds, lunch near Modia village. Evening arrival at Castle Bijaipur.
Day 08: Rest day at Bijaipur. Relax & rejuvenate. Enjoy activities at the Castle.
Day 09: Depart. Complimentary transfers to Chittor Station or Udaipur Airport.

Trekking Package

The tour begins with a bus/own vehicle departure from Rawat Ka Talab. Upon arrival, guests will visit a traditional village and explore the surrounding agricultural fields and farms. This region is well known for opium cultivation. During the opium harvesting season (December to the end of January), guests may have the opportunity to observe opium farming activities, subject to seasonal availability.

If the visit coincides with school hours and permission is available, the group will also visit a local village school (subject to school timings).

The safari experience will be conducted on foot and by track, covering the natural surroundings and village landscapes, with an approximate duration of 4 to 5 hours.

The journey then continues through the scenic Vindhya Valley forest area towards Pangarh. If lunch is arranged, the group will enjoy lunch at Pangarh. If lunch is not included, the group will visit Pangarh Lake, followed by high tea.

After the visit, guests will drive to Bijaipur by bus or cab. During the journey, a hotel jeep, hotel staff, and the driver will join and assist the group.

Package includes: Bus transportation (own vehicle), one backup vehicle, hotel staff assistance, driver, high tea, optional lunch.
