Welcome to Castle Bijaipur

Nestled in the scenic Vindhya Valley of Rajasthan, Castle Bijaipur is a heritage property that offers an authentic royal experience. Set amidst rolling hills, lush farmlands, and rich cultural landscapes, our palace invites you to step back in time while enjoying modern comforts.

From sunrise yoga sessions to thrilling jeep safaris, from hands-on cooking classes to serene cycling trails — every moment at Bijaipur is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime.

Curated Experiences

Discover the many ways to immerse yourself in the beauty of Bijaipur

Traditional Rajasthani Cooking Class

Learn the art of authentic Rajasthani cuisine from our expert chefs. From grinding spices to crafting traditional dishes, experience the flavors of royal kitchens firsthand.

Countryside Cycling

Pedal through the picturesque countryside of Bijaipur. Our guided cycling tours take you past ancient temples, vibrant villages, and sweeping farmlands — a perfect way to connect with the land.

Jeep Safari Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating jeep safari through the rugged terrain of the Vindhya Valley. Spot wildlife, traverse forest trails, and witness breathtaking sunsets from vantage points accessible only by 4x4.

Trekking & Nature Walks

Explore the untouched wilderness on foot. Our guided treks take you through ancient forests, along mountain ridges, and past hidden waterfalls — revealing the raw beauty of rural Rajasthan.

Yoga & Meditation

Begin your mornings with guided yoga and meditation sessions amidst the serenity of the palace grounds. Our experienced instructors help you find inner peace while surrounded by nature's calm.
