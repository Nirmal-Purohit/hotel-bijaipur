Welcome to Castle Bijaipur

Nestled in the scenic Vindhya Valley of Rajasthan, Castle Bijaipur is a heritage property that offers an authentic royal experience. Set amidst rolling hills, lush farmlands, and rich cultural landscapes, our palace invites you to step back in time while enjoying modern comforts.

From sunrise yoga sessions to thrilling jeep safaris, from hands-on cooking classes to serene cycling trails — every moment at Bijaipur is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime. The castle offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation for discerning travelers seeking an experience beyond the ordinary.

Traditional Rajasthani Cooking Class

Learn the art of authentic Rajasthani cuisine from our expert chefs. From grinding spices on traditional stone slabs to crafting dishes passed down through generations, experience the flavors of royal kitchens firsthand. Each session is hands-on, intimate, and ends with enjoying the meal you prepared.

The Castle & Accommodation

Castle Bijaipur is a 360-year-old heritage property, now a classified Heritage Hotel run by the local ruling family. The castle offers elegantly appointed rooms that blend royal Rajasthani decor with modern comforts — each space telling its own story of history and hospitality.

Bijaipur Horse Safari

Established in the late eighties by the owners of Castle Bijaipur, the Bijaipur Horse Safari offers exclusive horseback holidays through the countryside of South Rajasthan (Mewar). From humble safari tents under the starlit sky to the sophisticated comforts of Castles — quality horseback riding services with expert guides through India's heartland.

Sample Itinerary — Beginner Style (9 Days)

1

Day 01: Arrive Bijaipur

Arrive before lunch. Traditional welcome. Evening: first ride (2-hr local ride or lesson in castle grounds). Dinner & overnight at Castle Bijaipur (360-year-old Heritage Hotel).

2

Day 02–04: Riding Lessons & Fun

Ride mornings & evenings, free afternoons. Expert guides for beginners; experienced riders go further afield. Afternoons: cycling, cooking, massage, pool. Dine under the stars. Village tours in vintage jeeps.

3

Day 05: Bijaipur → Motipura (20 km, 5–6 hrs)

Through Eastern Highlands with lush fields amidst hills. Lunch at Damru. Ride through Guner flatlands. Overnight in Mughal-style safari tents at Motipura Lake.

4

Day 06: Motipura → Pangarh (21 km, 7 hrs)

Ride south through least inhabited area. Lunch at old Rawat lake. Forests, deserted village of Bhagwanpura. Pangarh Lake — 1000-year-old manmade lake, fort ruins, temples, birdwatcher's paradise. Overnight at Pangarh Lake Retreat.

5

Day 07: Pangarh → Bijaipur (23 km, 6 hrs)

Through valleys moving north. Amazing riding grounds. Lunch near Modia village. Evening arrival at Castle Bijaipur.

6

Day 08–09: Rest & Departure

Day 08: Relax & rejuvenate at Castle. Day 09: Depart. Complimentary transfers to Chittor Station or Udaipur Airport.

Jeep Safari Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating jeep safari through the rugged terrain of the Vindhya Valley. Spot wildlife, traverse forest trails, and witness breathtaking sunsets from vantage points accessible only by 4x4.

Trekking, Bird Watching & Village Excursion

Explore the untouched wilderness on foot. Our guided treks take you through ancient forests, along mountain ridges, and past hidden streams — including full-day village and Pangarh excursions through the scenic Vindhya Valley.

Village & Pangarh Excursion Itinerary

1

Departure

Bus/own vehicle from Rawat Ka Talab. Hotel staff and driver accompany throughout.

2

Village Visit

Traditional village, agricultural fields. Opium farming viewable Dec–Jan (subject to availability). School visit if timing permits.

3

Walking Safari

4–5 hours on foot and by track through natural surroundings and village landscapes.

4

Pangarh & Return

Through Vindhya Valley forest to Pangarh Lake. High tea or lunch at Pangarh. Return to Bijaipur by bus/cab with hotel staff.
