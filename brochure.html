Welcome to Castle Bijaipur

Nestled in the scenic Vindhya Valley of Rajasthan, Castle Bijaipur is a heritage property that offers an authentic royal experience. Set amidst rolling hills, lush farmlands, and rich cultural landscapes, our palace invites you to step back in time while enjoying modern comforts.

From sunrise yoga sessions to thrilling jeep safaris, from hands-on cooking classes to serene cycling trails — every moment at Bijaipur is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime. The castle offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation for discerning travelers seeking an experience beyond the ordinary.

Traditional Rajasthani Cooking Class

Learn the art of authentic Rajasthani cuisine from our expert chefs. From grinding spices on traditional stone slabs to crafting dishes passed down through generations, experience the flavors of royal kitchens firsthand. Each session is hands-on, intimate, and ends with enjoying the meal you prepared.

Countryside Cycling

Pedal through the picturesque countryside of Bijaipur on our guided cycling tours. The routes take you past ancient temples, vibrant villages, and sweeping farmlands — offering a unique perspective of rural Rajasthan that few travelers get to witness. Suitable for all fitness levels with multiple route options available.

Jeep Safari Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating jeep safari through the rugged terrain of the Vindhya Valley. Our experienced guides navigate you through dense forests, open plains, and rocky trails. Spot wildlife in their natural habitat, traverse ancient pathways, and witness breathtaking sunsets from vantage points accessible only by 4x4.

Trekking & Nature Walks

Explore the untouched wilderness on foot. Our guided treks take you through ancient forests, along mountain ridges, and past hidden streams — revealing the raw beauty of rural Rajasthan that remains unchanged for centuries. Each trek is tailored to the group's pace, with options ranging from gentle nature walks to full-day expeditions through the Vindhya hills.

Yoga & Meditation

Begin your mornings with guided yoga and meditation sessions amidst the serenity of the palace grounds. Our experienced instructors offer sessions for all levels — from gentle stretches to advanced asanas. The tranquil setting of the castle gardens provides the perfect backdrop for mindfulness and rejuvenation.

Village & Pangarh Excursion

A full-day immersive experience through the heart of traditional Rajasthan. Walk through living villages, witness ancient farming practices, and journey through the scenic Vindhya Valley forest to the serene Pangarh Lake.

Full Day Experience 4–5 Hours Walking Safari Min. 6 Guests
1

Departure

Bus or own vehicle departure from Rawat Ka Talab. Hotel staff and driver accompany the group throughout.

2

Village Visit

Explore a traditional village with agricultural fields and farms. This region is known for opium cultivation — viewable during harvest season (December to January), subject to availability.

3

Village School

Visit a local village school if timing and permission allow (subject to school hours).

4

Walking Safari

4–5 hours on foot and by track through natural surroundings, village landscapes, and forest trails.

5

Pangarh Visit

Journey through Vindhya Valley forest to Pangarh. Enjoy lunch at Pangarh (if arranged) or visit Pangarh Lake followed by high tea.

6

Return Journey

Drive to Bijaipur by bus or cab. Hotel jeep, staff, and driver join and assist the group.
